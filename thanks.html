How can we help?

Dovetail Project Solutions offers a comprehensive range of services for both residential and commercial needs.

  •  Yard maintenance; lawn & acreage mowing/slashing, whipper snipping & edging, hedge trimming, pruning of small trees/shrubs, gutter cleaning and rubbish removal.
  • Carpentry & Building; general repair and maintenance (hinges, handles, latches and locks on doors or gates, door & window framing, skirting boards, noisy steps), installation of new handrails, partition walls, shelving, building and fence structures (framing, brick or block work and concreting). 

    We can also fabricate and finish the inside and outside of structures using a variety of substrates.
  • Cabinet Making; custom storage solutions, kitchen cabinets and wardrobes and fit outs for the office and home.
  • Insurance Repairs; water and rising damp damage.
  • Plastering & Painting; internal & external ceilings, walls, patching of holes or filling of cracks.
  • Flooring; new decks and extensions, replacement of broken tiles or general polish and upkeep.
  • Solid Surface Bench Top; vanity or counter installation & repair. Certified Master Craftsman in DuPoint Corian Solid Surfaces.
  • Relocation Repairs; filling of cash register and computer cable cut outs, re-sanding and polishing and general wear and tear. 
